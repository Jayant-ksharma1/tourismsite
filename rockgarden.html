Rock Garden of Chandigarh

The Rock Garden of Chandigarh is a one-of-a-kind garden made entirely from industrial and home waste materials. It was created by Nek Chand, a former government employee, and was discovered in 1957. The garden features a wide variety of sculptures, waterfalls, and intricate pathways. It's not just a garden, but a piece of living art built from discarded materials.

History

The Rock Garden was created in secret, with Nek Chand working on it without the knowledge of his superiors. After its discovery, the government initially wanted to demolish it but later decided to preserve it as a unique cultural site. It is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most visited attractions in Chandigarh.

Interesting Facts

  • It was constructed over 18 years, and Nek Chand worked on it without any formal training in art.
  • More than 5000 sculptures are on display at the site.
  • Visitors can explore maze-like pathways and discover hidden areas filled with creativity.
