About Chandigarh

Chandigarh, often referred to as the 'City Beautiful,' is a modern and planned city located in northern India. It is the capital of both Punjab and Haryana. Known for its beautiful architecture, clean surroundings, and vibrant culture, Chandigarh is a blend of tradition and modernity.

The city is famous for its wide roads, green spaces, and unique architecture designed by Pierre Jeanneret and the famous architect Le Corbusier. Some popular attractions include the Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Rose Garden, Elante Mall, Punjab University, numerous treks, and the Sector 17 Plaza.
