Chandigarh Heritage Tour

Discover the beauty and history of Chandigarh's iconic landmarks.

Sukhna Lake

Sukhna Lake is a serene artificial reservoir located at the foothills of the Himalayas, perfect for boating, birdwatching, or simply enjoying a relaxing stroll. It spans over 3 kilometers and offers a peaceful escape from the city.

  • Boating
  • Cycling
  • Jogging
  • Birdwatching

Rock Garden

The Rock Garden of Chandigarh is an extraordinary sculpture garden made from recycled materials like broken ceramics, glass, and stones. Created by Nek Chand, it's a unique blend of art and nature, offering visitors an immersive experience.

  • Sculptural Figures
  • Waterfalls
  • Caves
  • Recycled Art Installations

Capitol Complex

The Capitol Complex is a UNESCO World Heritage site, designed by Le Corbusier. It houses the Legislative Assembly, High Court, and Secretariat and stands as a symbol of modernist architecture and urban planning.

  • The High Court
  • Legislative Assembly
  • Secretariat
  • Open Hand Monument
