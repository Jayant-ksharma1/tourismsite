Rock Garden

Visit the unique Rock Garden of Chandigarh, a stunning art installation created entirely from industrial & urban waste.

Sukhna Lake

Enjoy a tranquil boat ride or a peaceful walk along the scenic Sukhna Lake, a man-made reservoir in the heart of Chandigarh.

Rose Garden

Explore the Zakir Hussain Rose Garden, one of Asia's largest rose gardens, with thousands of rose varieties and lush greenery.

Hand Monument

Admire the Hand Monument, a symbolic structure representing unity, located at Sector 34, Chandigarh.

Capitol Complex

Discover the Capitol Complex, a UNESCO World Heritage Site designed by Pierre Jeanneret and Le Corbusier, showcasing modernist architecture and the seat of Chandigarh's government.
